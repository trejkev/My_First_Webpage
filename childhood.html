Childhood: Guarari downtown

Then my dad came to visit sometimes his aunt and then the magic came up when my dad finally met my mom in that neighborhood, 18 years old was my dad, 20 years old my mom, a beauty curly-haired woman that soon fell in love with a green-eyed man.
